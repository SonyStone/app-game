Props preserve reactivity

A component receives a props object through a call like Comp(props). When a prop comes from a signal or another getter, Solid tries to keep that access lazy instead of eagerly reading it at the call site.


In simplified terms, JSX like <Comp rotate={props.subsecond} becomes a getter on the props object. The component is then invoked through untrack, which avoids creating an accidental subscription just because the component was called.
